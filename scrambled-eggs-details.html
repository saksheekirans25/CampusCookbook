Simple Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in egg mixture and stir gently until cooked through, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately, enjoy!
